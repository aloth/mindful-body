**Train. Track. Transform.** Build workout routines, run habits and streaks, log every measurement, and watch one number — your **Body Score** — capture how it all comes together.

Mindful Body is the privacy-first workout tracker, habit tracker, and body composition app for iPhone, iPad, and Mac (via iPad compatibility). Full Apple Health integration, iCloud sync, and zero ads.

Part of the **Mindful Apps** family, alongside [Mindful Coffee](https://github.com/aloth/mindful-coffee).

## Why Mindful Body?

Most fitness apps make you choose: workout logger, habit tracker, or body-comp app. Mindful Body is all three in one privacy-first package, tied together by a single number that tells you how it's actually going. Whether you're cutting, bulking, building strength, or just staying consistent, Mindful Body shows you the full picture and the next move.

## Key Features

### 🏋️ Workouts That Grow With You

- **Routines** — Build them from a curated exercise library or start from sample studio plans
- **Live Sessions** — Run workouts and check off sets one by one
- **Smart Progression** — Equipment-aware suggestions based on your last performance (barbell, dumbbell, machine, cable, kettlebell, bodyweight)
- **Drop Sets** — Stagnation detected? The app suggests a drop set with −20 % weight, right in your session. Tap to add.
- **RIR Badges** — Set target Reps in Reserve per exercise; a color-coded badge keeps intensity honest during your workout.
- **Stagnation Detection** — Automatic plateau alerts when your top weight hasn't moved in multiple sessions.
- **Volume Sparkline** — See training volume trends per exercise
- **Lean-Mass Cross-Insights** — How your training tracks against body composition over time
- **Metric or Imperial** — Switch any time, history stays consistent (storage in kg, display in your unit)

### 🔥 Habits & Streaks

- **Three Modes** — Avoid (e.g. "No alcohol"), Do It (e.g. "Daily walk"), or Measure (e.g. "10,000 steps")
- **Six Categories** — Nutrition, recovery, movement, mindset, substance, custom
- **Curated Templates** — Start fast with proven habit definitions
- **Streaks** — Reward consistency without punishing slip-ups
- **HealthKit Bridge** — Pull in steps, mindful minutes, sunscreen and more

### 💯 Your Body Score — A Single Number

- **0 to 100 Score** — Combines body fat %, waist-to-height, and lean mass index
- **Age- and Sex-Aware** — Calibrated against Gallagher (2000) and AHA references
- **30-Day Sparkline** — See real movement, not smoothed estimates
- **Honest Tier Feedback** — Athletic, healthy, average, watch, risk
- **No Component Penalty** — Missing inputs reduce the denominator, not your score

### 📊 Complete Body Tracking

- **Weight & BMI** — Log weight with automatic BMI calculation based on your height
- **Body Composition** — Track body fat percentage, muscle mass, visceral fat level, and basal metabolic rate
- **Circumferences** — Chest, waist, hips, neck, arms, and thighs in one place
- **Smart Units** — Seamlessly switch between metric (kg/cm) and imperial (lbs/in)

### 📈 Insights & Trends

- **Now vs. Trends** — A "Today" snapshot plus a separate "Trends" view for long-term movement
- **Trend Charts** — Interactive charts for every metric with customizable time ranges
- **Selectable KPI Chart** — Tap a stat card on Today to swap the chart instantly
- **Science-Backed Insights** — Cited explanations for body recomposition, metabolic changes, and circumference trends
- **Logging Streaks** — Stay motivated with streak tracking and milestone celebrations
- **Goal-Aware Dashboard** — Cards turn green when you're on track toward your targets

### 🎯 Goal Setting

- **Flexible Goals** — Set targets for weight, body fat, waist circumference, or any tracked metric
- **Deadlines** — Optional deadline tracking to keep you accountable
- **Achievement Tracking** — Celebrate when you hit your goals

### 📸 Progress Photos

- **Face ID Protected** — Photos stay private with biometric authentication
- **Pose-Aware** — Organize by front, back, side-left, and side-right
- **Before & After** — Visual progress alongside your measurements
- **iCloud Sync** — Photos sync securely across your devices via CloudKit

### 💊 Supplement Tracking

- **Custom Supplements** — Create your own (protein, creatine, vitamins) with default doses
- **Timing Hints** — Set when to take each supplement (morning, before workout, after workout, before bed, more)
- **Flexible Schedules** — Daily, specific days, every N days, cycles, or pause until a chosen date
- **One-Tap Logging** — Quick-log default doses or enter custom amounts
- **Smart Status** — See what's due today, already taken, not scheduled, or paused
- **Per-Supplement Reminders** — Personal notification time for each supplement
- **Unified History** — Measurements and supplements in one timeline

### ❤️ Apple Health Integration

- **Two-Way Sync** — Read from and write to Apple Health automatically
- **Auto-Sync** — Checks Apple Health every 30 seconds while the app is active
- **Import History** — Pull up to 365 days of weight, body fat, lean mass, and waist data from HealthKit
- **Multiple Metrics** — Weight, body fat, BMI, lean body mass, waist circumference, BMR, steps, mindful minutes

### ☁️ iCloud Sync

- **Seamless Multi-Device** — Measurements, goals, workouts, habits, and progress photos sync via CloudKit
- **Sync Status Dashboard** — See sync status, last sync time, and force sync on demand
- **Deduplication Tool** — Built-in maintenance to clean up duplicate records

### 🔔 Smart Reminders

- **Daily Weigh-In** — Gentle nudge to step on the scale each morning
- **Weekly Check-In** — Reminder for comprehensive body composition logging
- **Monthly Progress Photo** — Don't forget to capture your visual progress
- **Habit Reminders** — Per-habit nudges with custom times
- **Sunscreen Reminder** — Tied to weather conditions

### 🧭 Five Clear Tabs

**Today** (right-now snapshot) · **Trends** (over-time analysis) · **Log** (everything you can record) · **Photos** (visual progress) · **Goals** (targets and achievements)

### 🌍 Localization

- **6 Languages** — English, German, Spanish, French, Japanese, Chinese (Simplified)

## Privacy

Mindful Body takes your privacy seriously:

- **No camera roll access for saving** — Progress photos live in the app's private container, never in your photo library
- **Face ID protection** — Progress photos can be locked behind biometric authentication
- **On-device processing** — All Body Score and trend calculations happen locally
- **No analytics, no tracking, no ads** — Zero third-party SDKs
- **iCloud encryption** — Synced data is encrypted in transit and at rest
- **Your data is yours** — Full data export capability
