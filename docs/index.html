Everything You Need to Track Your Progress

Comprehensive body composition tracking with smart insights and privacy-first design

🏋️

Workouts That Grow With You

Build routines from a curated exercise library, run live sessions and check off sets one by one. Smart, equipment-aware progression suggests your next jump based on your last performance. Volume sparkline and lean-mass cross-insights tie training to body composition.

🔥

Habits & Streaks

Three modes — Avoid, Do It, or Measure. Six categories from nutrition to mindset. Streaks reward consistency without punishing slip-ups. HealthKit bridges steps, mindful minutes, and more so qualifying habits update on their own.

💯

Your Body Score

A single number from 0 to 100 that combines body fat, waist-to-height and lean mass. Age- and sex-aware (Gallagher 2000 / AHA). 30-day sparkline shows real movement, not smoothed estimates. Honest tiers: athletic, healthy, average, watch, risk.

⚖️

Complete Body Tracking

Weight, body fat percentage, muscle mass, BMR, visceral fat, and circumferences (waist, hips, chest, neck, arms, thighs) in one place. Metric or imperial, switch any time.

📈

Now vs. Trends

Today shows your right-now snapshot. Trends shows movement over time. Selectable KPI chart, scope-stable highlights, and cited insights explain what your numbers actually mean.

🎯

Goal Setting

Set targets for weight, body fat, waist, or any tracked metric. Optional deadlines. Visual progress and achievement celebrations.

📸

Progress Photos

Capture your transformation with Face ID protection, pose-aware organization (front, back, side-left, side-right), and side-by-side comparisons over time.

💊

Supplement Tracking

Custom supplements with default doses, timing hints, and flexible schedules. Daily, specific days, every N days, cycles or pause until a chosen date. Clear due, taken, and paused states.

🍎

Apple Health Integration

Two-way HealthKit sync. Auto-checks every 30 seconds while the app is active. Imports up to 365 days of weight, body fat, lean mass, and waist history. Pulls steps and mindful minutes for habits.

☁️

iCloud Sync

Measurements, goals, workouts, habits, supplements, and progress photos sync via CloudKit across iPhone, iPad and modern Macs (via iPad compatibility).

🛡️

Privacy First

All data stays on your device or in your private iCloud. No analytics, no tracking, no ads. Body Score and trend math run on-device. Your body data is your business.

Everything you need to know about Mindful Body

The Body Score is a single number from 0 to 100 that combines body fat percentage, waist-to-height ratio and lean mass index. It is age- and sex-aware (calibrated against Gallagher 2000 / AHA references) and includes a 30-day trend bonus. Honest tiers: Athletic 85+, Healthy 70–84, Average 55–69, Watch 40–54, Risk under 40.

Build routines from a curated exercise library or start from sample studio plans, then run a live session and check off sets one by one. After each session the app suggests the next jump using equipment-aware increments (2.5 kg for barbell, machine, cable; 1.0 kg for dumbbell, kettlebell). Metric or imperial display, kg storage so history stays consistent.

Habits come in three modes: Avoid (e.g. "No alcohol"), Do It (e.g. daily walk), or Measure (e.g. 10,000 steps). Pick from curated templates across nutrition, recovery, movement, mindset and substance categories or create your own. Streaks reward consistency without punishing slip-ups, and HealthKit can update qualifying habits automatically.

A comprehensive set of body metrics: weight, body fat percentage, muscle mass, BMR, visceral fat level, and circumferences (waist, hips, chest, arms, neck, thighs). Plus workouts, habits, supplements and progress photos. Log as many or as few as you like.

Absolutely. Progress photos are protected by Face ID or Touch ID, stored in the app's private container (never in your camera roll), and synced only via your private iCloud account (CloudKit). Never shared with third parties.

Yes. iCloud / CloudKit syncs measurements, goals, workouts, habits, supplements, and progress photos across iPhone, iPad and modern Macs via iPad compatibility, as long as you are signed into the same Apple ID.

Yes. Two-way HealthKit integration reads weight, body fat, lean mass, waist, steps and mindful minutes from Apple Health, auto-checks every 30 seconds while the app is active, imports up to 365 days of history in one tap, and writes your logged data back to Health.
