Everything you need to know about Mindful Body

Mindful Body supports a comprehensive set of body metrics: weight, body fat percentage, muscle mass, BMR (Basal Metabolic Rate), visceral fat level, and body circumferences (waist, hips, chest, arms, legs, and more). You can log as many or as few as you like — the app works with any smart scale or manual entry.

Absolutely. Progress photos are protected by Face ID or Touch ID — you need to authenticate to view them. Photos are stored securely on your device and synced via your private iCloud account (CloudKit). They are never shared with third parties and never leave your personal iCloud storage.

Yes! Mindful Body uses iCloud / CloudKit for seamless multi-device sync. All your measurements, goals, and photos stay in sync across your iPhone, iPad, and Mac automatically — as long as you're signed into the same Apple ID.

No smart scale required! You can manually enter any metric directly in the app. If you do have a smart scale that syncs with Apple Health, Mindful Body can automatically read those measurements via HealthKit integration — making logging effortless.

Yes — Mindful Body has two-way HealthKit integration. It reads body measurements from Apple Health (great if your scale syncs there) and writes your logged data back to Apple Health, keeping your health ecosystem complete and up to date.
